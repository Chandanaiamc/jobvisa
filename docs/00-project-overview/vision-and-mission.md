# Vision and Mission

**JobVisa.lk**  
Sri Lanka’s enterprise platform for overseas employment  
Official product vision document for investors, partners, and future team members

---

## 1. Vision Statement

To become the most trusted gateway for Sri Lankans to build dignified careers abroad — and the preferred hiring channel for verified overseas employers and licensed recruitment agencies seeking reliable Sri Lankan talent.

---

## 2. Mission Statement

JobVisa.lk connects Sri Lankan job seekers with verified overseas employers and licensed recruitment agencies through a secure, transparent, and professionally managed employment marketplace.

We exist to make overseas opportunity clearer, safer, and more accessible — reducing exploitation, improving match quality, and helping people move from aspiration to employment with confidence.

---

## 3. Core Values

1. **Trust First**  
   Every listing, employer, and agency relationship must earn confidence. We do not trade trust for short-term growth.

2. **Dignity of Work**  
   Overseas employment is a life decision. We treat candidates as people with futures, not as volume in a pipeline.

3. **Transparency**  
   Clear roles, clear processes, and honest communication between seekers, employers, and agencies.

4. **Verification Culture**  
   We prioritize verified employers and licensed recruitment partners over unverified reach.

5. **Fair Access**  
   Opportunity should not depend on privileged networks alone. We design for broad, equitable access across Sri Lanka.

6. **Quality Over Noise**  
   Better matches matter more than more listings. We optimize for outcomes, not empty activity.

7. **Accountability**  
   Platform participants are responsible for their conduct. We enforce standards that protect the marketplace.

8. **Long-Term Partnership**  
   We build for lasting relationships with employers, agencies, and talent — not one-off transactions.

9. **National Responsibility**  
   Overseas employment shapes families and remittances. We operate with awareness of that national impact.

10. **Continuous Excellence**  
    We improve relentlessly — in service quality, marketplace integrity, and professional standards.

## 4. Long-term Goals (5 Years)

1. **Establish category leadership in Sri Lanka**  
   Become the recognized primary digital destination for overseas job discovery and verified hiring connections.

2. **Build a high-trust verified network**  
   Scale a substantial base of verified overseas employers and licensed recruitment agencies actively hiring through the platform.

3. **Deliver meaningful employment outcomes**  
   Enable a large volume of successful applications and placements while maintaining quality and candidate protection standards.

4. **Create a sustainable marketplace business**  
   Achieve strong recurring revenue from employer and agency value — with healthy unit economics and reinvestment capacity.

5. **Become an employer brand of choice**  
   Attract and retain exceptional talent in product, trust & safety, partnerships, and customer success across Sri Lanka.

---

## 5. Long-term Goals (10 Years)

1. **Define the standard for ethical overseas recruitment digitally**  
   Set the benchmark in Sri Lanka for verified, transparent, and candidate-respecting overseas employment platforms.

2. **Expand regional influence**  
   Extend JobVisa.lk’s model and partnerships across key destination markets and complementary talent corridors, while remaining rooted in Sri Lankan excellence.

3. **Operate as critical national infrastructure for overseas careers**  
   Be regarded by candidates, agencies, employers, and institutions as essential digital infrastructure for overseas employment pathways.

4. **Build a multi-product overseas careers ecosystem**  
   Grow beyond job matching into a broader careers platform — readiness, trusted information, and long-term professional mobility support.

5. **Create enduring institutional value**  
   Establish JobVisa.lk as a durable, well-governed company with strong brand equity, trusted data stewardship, and multi-decade relevance.

## 6. Problems We Solve

**For job seekers**
- Fragmented and unreliable information about overseas jobs  
- Exposure to unverified agents, misleading offers, and unsafe processes  
- Difficulty presenting skills professionally to international employers  
- Lack of visibility into application progress and legitimate opportunities  

**For overseas employers**
- Hard-to-reach, hard-to-verify access to Sri Lankan talent  
- Inconsistent candidate quality and incomplete profiles  
- Inefficient hiring channels with weak accountability  

**For licensed recruitment agencies**
- Limited digital reach and brand trust at scale  
- Manual, fragmented workflows between candidates and overseas principals  
- Competition from informal and non-compliant channels  

**For the market as a whole**
- Low transparency and uneven professional standards  
- Trust gaps that slow legitimate hiring and harm candidates  
- Absence of a modern, enterprise-grade platform purpose-built for Sri Lanka’s overseas employment reality

## 7. Our Competitive Advantage

1. **Sri Lanka–native focus**  
   Built specifically for Sri Lankan seekers, local market realities, and the overseas employment journey — not a generic global job board adaptation.

2. **Verification-led marketplace**  
   Emphasis on verified overseas employers and licensed recruitment agencies as a core product principle, not a marketing slogan.

3. **Two-sided professional design**  
   Equally serious about candidate experience and employer/agency workflows — creating a true marketplace, not a one-sided listing site.

4. **Trust as a product**  
   Moderation, accountability, and transparent processes are treated as central value, not afterthoughts.

5. **Enterprise ambition with local empathy**  
   Institutional-grade platform thinking combined with deep understanding of Sri Lankan aspirations, constraints, and mobile-first behavior.

6. **Long-term category ownership mindset**  
   We are building durable infrastructure for overseas employment — not chasing short-lived traffic spikes.

## 8. Why JobVisa.lk Exists

Millions of Sri Lankan families depend on overseas employment for income, stability, and upward mobility. Yet the path from interest to a legitimate overseas job is often unclear, uneven, and vulnerable to misuse.

JobVisa.lk exists to replace confusion with clarity, and risk with verified connection.

We believe Sri Lankan talent deserves a modern platform that respects their ambition. We believe overseas employers and licensed agencies deserve a reliable channel to hire with confidence. We believe the country’s overseas employment ecosystem deserves digital infrastructure worthy of its economic and human importance.

JobVisa.lk is our commitment to that future.

## 9. Success Metrics

We measure success by outcomes that matter to people and to the business:

| Area | What “good” looks like |
|---|---|
| **Trust** | High share of active jobs from verified employers / licensed agencies; low incident rates; strong user trust scores |
| **Marketplace health** | Growing active seekers, active employers/agencies, and quality job supply across priority destinations |
| **Matching effectiveness** | Strong apply-to-response rates; improving shortlist and hire conversion over time |
| **Candidate experience** | High profile completion; repeat usage; positive satisfaction with clarity and safety |
| **Employer/agency experience** | Time-to-shortlist improvement; retention of paying partners; referral-led partner growth |
| **Brand** | Unaided awareness as Sri Lanka’s go-to overseas jobs platform; strong employer brand for talent |
| **Business sustainability** | Predictable recurring revenue; healthy margins; efficient acquisition of both sides of the market |
| **Impact** | Documented successful overseas employment journeys enabled through the platform |
